# Design Principles

Svenska Spel's core design principles that guide all product decisions and create cohesive, engaging user experiences across all digital touchpoints.

---

## Introduction

We create greater experiences with less by ensuring every detail has a purpose and we opt out of things that do not add any value.

To deliver great user experiences in our products, it's important to have a good understanding of Svenska Spel's design language and approaches.

**Core Philosophy:**
> Every detail must serve a purpose. We choose to exclude anything that doesn't add value.

---

## Our Design Language

The Svenska Spel design language is built on three fundamental principles that work together to create distinctive, user-friendly experiences:

1. **Vi gör det enkelt** (We make it simple)
2. **Vi skapar dynamik** (We create dynamics)
3. **Vi är på riktigt** (We are genuine)

These principles inform every design decision, from overall product strategy down to the smallest interaction detail.

---

## Principle 1: Vi gör det enkelt (We Make It Simple)

Simplicity is the foundation required to create an attractive experience together with our other design principles.

### Simplicity Through Purpose

**Philosophy:**
We create more experience with less by ensuring that every single detail has a purpose, and we choose to exclude what doesn't add value. By offering fewer choices, we create an easy-to-navigate and clear environment where users feel confident and secure. When we scale back, what's most important—our games—gets more focus.

**Key Tenets:**
- Every detail must serve a purpose
- Fewer choices create clarity and confidence
- Scaling back gives focus to what matters most
- Simplicity enables other design principles to shine

**In Practice:**
- Remove unnecessary options and features
- Streamline navigation paths
- Focus user attention on primary actions
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Purposeful use of space

---

### Consistency Through Cohesion

**Philosophy:**
We build one Svenska Spel through consistent use of central design elements and style guides. Cohesive use of layout, design elements, typography, and interaction functions means users feel secure and easily navigate because the experience works the same everywhere. We look at the entire user experience and ensure that every step seamlessly contributes to the whole.

**Key Tenets:**
- Unified design system across all products
- Consistent patterns build user confidence
- Every step contributes to the whole experience
- Coherent brand expression throughout

**In Practice:**
- Use established component patterns
- Maintain consistent navigation structure
- Apply design tokens consistently
- Unified interaction patterns
- Seamless cross-product experiences

**Benefits:**
- Users feel secure and oriented
- Reduced learning curve
- Predictable interactions
- Strong brand recognition
- Efficient product development

---

## Principle 2: Vi skapar dynamik (We Create Dynamics)

Dynamics bring our digital experiences to life through motion, engagement, and progression.

### Motion and Animation

**Philosophy:**
We create dynamics and engaging content through smart use of animation and moving images. This helps us show distinction, explain functions, and enhance interactivity in both navigation and games. Motion is also one of our most important tools for conveying personality, humor, and presence.

**Key Tenets:**
- Animation shows distinction and differentiation
- Motion explains functions and guides users
- Movement enhances interactivity
- Animation conveys personality and emotion
- Purposeful, not decorative motion

**In Practice:**
- Micro-interactions provide feedback
- Transitions guide user attention
- Loading states maintain engagement
- Animated illustrations add personality
- Motion reinforces brand character

**Animation Purposes:**
1. **Functional** - Guide attention, show relationships, explain actions
2. **Feedback** - Confirm actions, show progress, indicate states
3. **Personality** - Express brand character, create delight, add humor
4. **Continuity** - Smooth transitions, maintain context, reduce jarring changes

---

### Progressive Experience

**Philosophy:**
We create an experience that grows with the player by lowering thresholds, acknowledging experts, and constantly offering new challenges. We are guiding by clearly showing where the user is located and helping them both find what they're looking for and discover new parts of Svenska Spel. We always do this with strong brand presence and a tonality that makes the user feel seen.

**Key Tenets:**
- Lower barriers for new users
- Acknowledge and support experts
- Continuous discovery and challenge
- Clear wayfinding and orientation
- Users feel recognized and seen

**In Practice:**
- Onboarding flows for new users
- Progressive disclosure of complexity
- Expert modes and shortcuts
- Clear navigation breadcrumbs
- Personalized recommendations
- Skill-based challenges

**User Journey Stages:**
1. **Beginner** - Simple, guided, supportive
2. **Intermediate** - More options, faster flows
3. **Expert** - Advanced features, shortcuts, customization

---

### Personalization and Tone

**Philosophy:**
For a more personal digital experience, we actively work with data-driven functionality. We see and acknowledge our players. Always with high transparency and respect for our players' privacy. We always use a human tonality that doesn't just inform but also creates emotions and builds relationships. Both in overall messages and down to the formulation in the smallest button.

**Key Tenets:**
- Data-driven personalization
- Recognize and acknowledge players
- Transparent about data use
- Respect for privacy
- Human, emotional communication
- Relationship building through language

**In Practice:**
- Personalized content and recommendations
- Recognition of user preferences
- Clear privacy controls and transparency
- Conversational, warm language
- Emotional resonance in messaging
- Consistent "Stämningshöjaren" personality

**Personalization Levels:**
1. **Content** - Relevant games and offers
2. **Interface** - Customized views and shortcuts
3. **Communication** - Personalized messages
4. **Experience** - Adaptive difficulty and pacing

## How Principles Work Together

The three design principles are interconnected and reinforce each other:

### Simplicity + Dynamics
- Simple foundations allow dynamics to shine
- Motion clarifies simple interfaces
- Animation makes simplicity engaging
- Clear hierarchy directs dynamic content

### Simplicity + Authenticity
- Simple, honest communication
- Transparent design choices
- Clear privacy and data practices
- Straightforward user paths

### Dynamics + Authenticity
- Genuine personality through motion
- Real-time, honest feedback
- Personalization respects individuality
- Human warmth in interactions

### All Three Together
Creating experiences that are:
- Easy to understand and use
- Engaging and delightful
- Honest and trustworthy
- Personal and human

## Application Guidelines

### Product Strategy

**Consider:**
- Does this feature serve a clear purpose? (Simplicity)
- Does it create engagement and progression? (Dynamics)
- Is it transparent and genuine? (Authenticity)
- How do the principles balance in this feature?

### Design Decisions

**Ask:**
- Is this the simplest solution that achieves the goal?
- Does motion/animation enhance understanding?
- Does the tone feel human and caring?
- Are we being transparent with users?

### Component Design

**Ensure:**
- Consistent with design system (Simplicity)
- Appropriate interactive states (Dynamics)
- Clear, honest communication (Authenticity)
- Purposeful, not decorative elements

## Examples in Practice

### Navigation
- **Simplicity:** Clear hierarchy, limited top-level options
- **Dynamics:** Smooth transitions, animated indicators
- **Authenticity:** Honest labels, clear current location

### Forms
- **Simplicity:** One task per screen, clear labels
- **Dynamics:** Real-time validation, progress indication
- **Authenticity:** Helpful error messages, transparent requirements

### Game Interfaces
- **Simplicity:** Focus on core gameplay, minimal distractions
- **Dynamics:** Engaging animations, responsive feedback
- **Authenticity:** Clear odds, honest information, responsible gaming

### Onboarding
- **Simplicity:** Step-by-step guidance, essential information only
- **Dynamics:** Progressive disclosure, interactive tutorials
- **Authenticity:** Transparent about what to expect, genuine help

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Over-Simplification
- ❌ Removing necessary features for "simplicity"
- ❌ Hiding important information
- ❌ Making expert users go through beginner flows
- ✓ Balance simplicity with functionality
- ✓ Use progressive disclosure appropriately

### Excessive Dynamics
- ❌ Animation for decoration only
- ❌ Motion that distracts or slows down
- ❌ Over-personalization that feels invasive
- ✓ Purposeful animation only
- ✓ Respect user privacy and preferences

### False Authenticity
- ❌ Fake personalization ("Hello, User!")
- ❌ Dishonest or unclear communication
- ❌ Hidden fees or conditions
- ✓ Genuine personalization with real data
- ✓ Transparent, honest communication
- ✓ Clear about costs and conditions

## Design Principle Checklist

Before launching a feature or product, verify:

### Simplicity
✓ Every element serves a clear purpose
✓ Unnecessary complexity removed
✓ Clear visual hierarchy
✓ Consistent with design system
✓ Users can easily navigate and understand

### Dynamics
✓ Engaging and interactive
✓ Motion serves a purpose
✓ Progressive experience for different skill levels
✓ Personality shines through
✓ Users discover new things

### Authenticity
✓ Transparent about data and privacy
✓ Honest, human communication
✓ Genuine personalization
✓ Respects user autonomy
✓ Builds trust and relationships

## Evolution and Adaptation

Design principles should be:
- **Stable** - Core philosophy remains constant
- **Adaptable** - Application evolves with technology and user needs
- **Measurable** - Track success through metrics
- **Teachable** - Easy for teams to understand and apply
- **Scalable** - Work at all levels from strategy to details

**Regular Review:**
- Quarterly principle application audits
- User feedback on principle alignment
- Team workshops on principle interpretation
- Continuous refinement of application guidelines

## Summary

**Three Core Principles:**

1. **Vi gör det enkelt (We Make It Simple)**
   - Purpose-driven design
   - Consistent experiences
   - Clear, focused interfaces

2. **Vi skapar dynamik (We Create Dynamics)**
   - Engaging motion and animation
   - Progressive, growing experiences
   - Personal, human interactions

3. **Vi är på riktigt (We Are Genuine)**
   - Transparent practices
   - Honest communication
   - Respectful personalization

**Together they create:**
Experiences that are simple to understand, engaging to use, and genuine in every interaction—making Svenska Spel a trusted, delightful digital environment for all users.
