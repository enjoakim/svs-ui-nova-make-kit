# Logotype

The Svenska Spel logotype system featuring the Vinnarkuben (Winner Cube) symbol and flexible relationship between symbol and wordmark.

---

## Philosophy

Our symbol has been a stylized winner in white on a red square since 1997. Our evolved symbol Vinnarkuben builds on recognition while making us both more visible and more entertaining. A cube, a perfect symbol for all sides of our rich offering. A "game piece" with more dimensions for more senses. Together with our typography, it creates dynamics, desire, and a greater feeling in our sender identity.

**Core Principles:**
- **Great flexibility** - Optimized for many different channels and surfaces
- **No fixed wordmark** - Flexible relationship between symbol and wordmark
- **Motion as a natural element** - Animation brings the brand to life
- **The cube leads** - Vinnarkuben is our most important asset

---

## Flexible Approach

We have a free and flexible relationship between the Winner Symbol and our name Svenska Spel. This creates optimal conditions for a clear sender identity regardless of occasion or contact surface. 

**Key Benefits:**
- Vinnarkuben is our most important asset and the one that most clearly shows it's us speaking
- We use it big and let it take up space
- Our name Svenska Spel doesn't have to stand alone but can just as easily be part of a sentence
- This creates optimal flexibility and the opportunity to be a living and interesting sender

**Implementation:**
The flexible approach enables different combinations where text can be placed overlapping behind or in front of the symbol. The expression can vary depending on context to create dynamics and contrast. This creates good conditions to increase the size of Vinnarkuben and make us clear as a sender.

## Vinnarkuben (Winner Cube)

With its three-dimensionality and free placement, Vinnarkuben adds energy and feeling to our communication.

### Available Versions

Vinnarkuben is available in both positive and negative versions, and is produced in the color systems RGB, CMYK, PANTONE, and black & white. This ensures that the logotype works in all contexts – from web and digital platforms to printed materials, as well as use in Word and PowerPoint.

### Usage Guidelines

**Preferred Version:**
Use primarily the version of Vinnarkuben that has different red tones on the sides. The other variants are exception solutions for occasions when you cannot use multiple colors for technical reasons.

**Rotation:**
If you want to angle Vinnarkuben slightly to the right or left, that's fine.

## Wordmark

Svenska Spel can just as easily be part of a sentence. This creates optimal flexibility and means we don't have to add a separate wordmark. Explore how this can make us as a sender more clear in the unit you're working with. Use our typefaces for this.

### Available Versions

The wordmark is available in:
- **Single-line version** - For horizontal layouts
- **Two-line version** - For vertical or compact layouts
- **Positive variant** - Dark text for light backgrounds
- **Negative variant** - Light text for dark backgrounds

All wordmarks are available in the color systems RGB, CMYK, PANTONE, and black & white, ensuring they work in all contexts – from web and digital platforms to printed materials, as well as use in Word and PowerPoint.

## Fixed Combinations

Try to first and foremost use the strength of the flexible relationship between Svenska Spel in text and Vinnarkuben. A bit more challenging perhaps, but often gives a better end result.

### Lockup Variants

For practical reasons, the logotype exists in some fixed lockups:

**Overlapping Variant (Preferred):**
The left variant where Vinnarkuben and the wordmark overlap the most is preferred if it fits the available surface.

**Traditional Variant:**
The rightmost variant is a bit more traditional and not as exciting.

**Negative Variants:**
All lockups are available in negative versions for use on dark backgrounds.

## General Guidelines

### Minimum Size

On smaller surfaces, it's important that the logotype is still large enough to be clearly seen and read. Remember that print quality or screen resolution determines readability and how small a size you can use.

**Always ensure:**
- Good readability
- Clear visibility
- Appropriate sizing for medium (print vs. digital)

### Clearance Space

The logotype sometimes appears alongside other information and graphics. To maintain its clarity in these situations, clearance space is defined.

**Clearance Space Definition:**
The clearance space equals the height of the letter "S" in the logotype. When the logotype size changes, the clearance space also changes – proportionally with the logotype.

**Purpose:**
Can be good to have as a guideline if you want the logotype to stand out.

## Don'ts

Avoid modifying – neither in form, color, effects, or with any other graphic attributes.

### Rotation
- ✗ **Don't** over-rotate the logotype

### Color
- ✗ **Don't** change the color of the logotype

### Effects
- ✗ **Don't** add effects such as shadows

### Shape
- ✗ **Don't** place the logotype inside another shape

### Distortion
- ✗ **Don't** distort the logotype

### Typography
- ✗ **Don't** write Svenska Spel in typefaces other than Svenska Spel's own

### Background
- ✗ **Don't** place the sender on busy backgrounds

### Contrast
- ✗ **Don't** ensure sufficient contrast for good readability
- ✗ **Don't** choose positive or negative wordmark so that it suits the background
